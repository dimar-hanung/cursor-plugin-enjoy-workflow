# Trust, Consent & Agency

Open for sharing, publishing, billing, privacy, permissions, notifications, AI output, automation, imports/bulk edits, or any action that can surprise the user.

## Do not surprise

Before high-stakes action, make visible: **what will happen**, **who/what is affected**, **whether/how to undo**, **why it's worth the cost** (money, trust, private data, time). If you can't summarize clearly, it's not ready for one click.

- **NEVER** send, publish, charge, delete, share, or sync without the user knowing it happened.
- **DO** preview before external impact — real audience, content, timing, permissions. **NEVER** generic "Are you sure?" (see writing.md).

## Consent

- **DO** ask at the moment it matters, not only at onboarding.
- **NEVER** bundle required agreement + marketing + tracking into one checkbox.
- **DO** name object, audience, destination, duration where relevant.
- **DO** make consent revocable where revocation is meaningful.

## AI & automation

- **DO** treat AI output as draft until the user accepts — preview, edit, regenerate, discard are distinct actions.
- **NEVER** auto-commit generated text/code/email/report to send, publish, charge, delete, or durable data without explicit approval when stakes are real.
- **DO** show automation states: pending, running, completed, failed, skipped, cancelled — with pause/cancel/undo when stakes justify.
- **DO** history/logs for actions that affect others or durable data; distinguish user vs system/automation actions.

## Permissions & billing

- **DO** ask smallest permission for the current task, in context, after value is visible.
- **DO** explain what permission enables in user terms — "Allow access" alone is not enough.
- **DO** show price, interval, renewal, tax/fees, cancellation effects before purchase.
- **NEVER** hide limits that create surprise cost; **DO** warn before trial converts or quota exceeded.
- **DON'T** hide cost, effort, risk, or limits behind friendly copy — respect beats conversion pressure.

## Imports & bulk edits

- **DO** preview imports and bulk changes before mutating durable data.
- **DO** show scope ("3 of 5 files uploaded — retry failed files") for partial failure.
